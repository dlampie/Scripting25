About Me

My name is Devin Lampier, and I am a student at Lethbridge Polytechnic. I am passionate about photography and travel, and I enjoy practicing graphic design in my spare time. My work often explores the intersection between nature and creativity, finding inspiration in the details that many overlook. I am part of a family business called TraveLLLers3 Fine Arts and Photography, where we share our artistic vision through images and stories from our adventures.

I have lived in Lethbridge my entire life and draw inspiration from the landscapes, wildlife, and people around me. Photography allows me to capture moments of beauty and stillness, while design helps me express those same emotions visually. Whether I am taking photos of local wildlife, designing digital pieces, or traveling to new places, I am always looking for ways to grow as an artist and storyteller.
