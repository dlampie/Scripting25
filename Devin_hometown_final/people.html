People

Explore the sections below to learn more about Lethbridge.

One of the best things about Lethbridge is the people who live here. The city is known for being warm, welcoming, and community-focused. Lethbridge has grown into a diverse place where residents come from many different cultural backgrounds, creating a strong sense of inclusion and belonging.

The municipal census shows that the fastest-growing areas of the city are the west and south sides, which attract a lot of young families and students (City of Lethbridge, 2023). Because the University of Lethbridge and Lethbridge College (formerly Lethbridge Polytechnic) are major parts of the community, education plays a huge role in local culture. Many residents work or study at these institutions, or participate in events and programs they offer.
