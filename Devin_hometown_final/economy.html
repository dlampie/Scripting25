Economy

Explore the sections below to learn more about Lethbridge.

Lethbridge has a strong and steadily growing economy. While agriculture has always been its foundation, the city has expanded into many different industries, including education, healthcare, retail, technology, and food processing. This makes the local economy well-rounded and resilient.

According to a Government of Canada Location Spotlight, Lethbridge is an important economic hub for southern Alberta and has easy access to both national and international markets (“Location Spotlight – Lethbridge, Alberta,” PrairiesCan). A large number of residents work in public services such as schools, hospitals, and city departments. Others are employed in farming, agricultural research, transportation, or private businesses.

In recent years, Lethbridge has also become more appealing to entrepreneurs and start-ups thanks to its supportive business environment and innovation-focused programs. This combination of traditional industries and new opportunities helps create reliable jobs and a healthy local economy.
